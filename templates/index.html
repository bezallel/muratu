HOW IT WORKS

Renting isn’t stressful because there aren’t enough options — it’s stressful because prices aren’t clear. Murami takes away the guesswork. We show you realistic rent ranges based on the type of home you want, in the areas you care about, so you can plan with confidence.

1. Tell Us What You Need
Answer a few simple questions — location, type of housing, number of rooms, etc.
2. Murami Calculates
Using a trained model on thousands of past listings and local rent behavior.
3. You Get a Realistic Range
Not just one price, but a range — see what’s normal, high, and low.
4. You Can Adjust & Explore
Change location, housing type, or other details to explore options.

ABOUT
“MURAMI”

Murami means “prepare me” in Yoruba — and that’s the mission: To help renters in Nigeria walk into their housing journey with clarity, not confusion.
The Model
Murami uses an XGBoost algorithm — chosen for its ability to perform well with limited or sparse datasets. It's effective at making accurate predictions even when information is incomplete, which is common in real estate data.
The model was trained on data scraped from apartment leasing platforms, with a focus on how different amenities and features influence rent prices.

Limitations:
While adept at historical estimates, this model doesn't adapt to immediate market shifts and may not capture current trends.

Note: Data used to train this model will not be updated to reflect evolving trends as they occur.


Murami is for the prepared renter — not the lucky one.
