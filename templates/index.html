      Project Focus: Solving Lagos Rental Uncertainty through Mùràmí.

      Searching for suitable apartments, particularly in the bustling city of Lagos, can be a daunting task, compounded by the uncertainty of whether the options align with your budget. Enter MÙRÁMÍ, your guide to a more informed renting experience. By factoring in your preferences for amenities, location, and housing type, Mùràmí offers an approximate annual rent estimate, enabling you to make more informed decisions before finalizing your housing choice.

      Model Description: XGBoost Algorithm and Data Advantage.

      The Mùràmí. project utilizes the XGBoost algorithm to address the distinct challenges presented by the dataset. Particularly, due to the dataset's limited nature, XGBoost was deliberately chosen for its remarkable performance in scenarios featuring restricted feature sets. The algorithm's capacity to generate accurate predictions even when dealing with limited information highlights its practicality. its ability to handle incomplete data strngthens its reliability. XGBoost's expertise in addressing the intricacies of restricted datasets positions it as a suitable approach for producing precise forecasts of housing prices, particularly focusing on the influence of diverse amenities. Furthermore, the data this model was trained on, was scraped from apartment leasing platforms.

      Limitations:
      While adept at historical estimates, this model doesn't adapt to immediate market shifts, and so, current trends might not be fully captured.

      Note: Data used to train this model will not be updated to reflect evolving trends as they occur.
